❓ Mod 常见问题Mod FAQ

Q: Mod安装后游戏闪退怎么办？Game crashes after installing mods?

A: 检查Bypasser版本兼容性，尝试移除最新安装的Mod逐个排查。A: Check Bypasser version compatibility, remove recent mods one by one.

Q: 使用Mod会被封号吗？Can I get banned for using mods?

A: NTE暂无明确Mod政策。皮肤类Mod风险较低，建议离线使用。A: NTE has no clear mod policy. Skin mods are low risk, use offline.

Q: 手机版能用Mod吗？Can mods be used on mobile?

A: 目前仅支持PC版。A: Currently only supports PC version.

Q: 游戏更新后Mod失效？Mods broken after update?

A: 等待作者更新兼容版本，检查Bypasser新版。A: Wait for author updates, check for new Bypasser version.

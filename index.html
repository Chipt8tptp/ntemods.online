Game Description

Supercar Legends is a dynamic runner game where math meets supercars! Drive through gates that multiply, divide, add or subtract the number of cars in your convoy. Collect as many cars as possible to defeat the boss at the end of each level.

How to Play

  • Use your mouse or finger to drag left and right to control your cars
  • Drive through gates with positive numbers to increase your convoy
  • Avoid gates with negative numbers that will reduce your cars
  • Defeat the boss at the end of each level to earn coins
  • Use coins to upgrade your cars and unlock new vehicles

Best Tips & Tricks

1. Always Choose Multiplication First

Multiplication gates give you the biggest increase in cars. Always prioritize ×2, ×3 and ×4 gates over addition gates. For example, ×2 is better than +5 when you have more than 5 cars.

2. Upgrade Order

Upgrade your cars in this order for maximum efficiency:

  1. Starting Cars (most important)
  2. Damage per Car
  3. Income Multiplier
  4. Car Speed

3. Boss Fight Strategy

Before reaching the boss, make sure you have at least 20-30 cars. The boss has a health bar, and each of your cars deals damage equal to your damage upgrade.

4. Watch Ads for Double Coins

After completing each level, you can watch a short ad to double your coins. This is the fastest way to earn coins and upgrade your cars.
